Our story
Literature belongs
to everyone
Global BookShelf was built on a single conviction — that the world's greatest stories should be accessible to every reader, in every language, from every culture. Not just the ones published in New York and London.
Why we exist
We built what we
couldn't find
There are over four million books in the world. Most reading platforms show you the same thousand. The algorithm recommends what sold well in English last month. The African novel that changed a generation sits undiscovered. The Japanese writer who influenced García Márquez has never been translated.
Global BookShelf exists to fix this. We built a living world map where every book is pinned to the place it came from. We built an AI that learns who you are — not just what genre you last clicked — and connects you to books that will change you. We built a translation platform where readers who speak Swahili or Yorùbá or Amharic can unlock literature that was never available in their language.
"The map doesn't show you what's popular. It shows you what's human."
We are a platform built by readers for readers. We do not take advertising money. We do not sell your data. We do not optimize for engagement metrics. We optimize for one thing: connecting you to the right book at the right moment in your life.

Fair Economy Model
Writers earn 95 cents
of every dollar
The traditional publishing industry gives authors 10–15% of the cover price. Literary agents take 15% of that. The author — the person who created everything — ends up with 8 cents of every dollar spent on their work.
We built the opposite. When a reader becomes a patron, 95% of their subscription goes directly to the author's bank account via Stripe Connect. Global BookShelf takes 5% to keep the lights on.

Privacy policy
Your reading life is
yours alone
Global BookShelf is built on a simple privacy principle — the books you read, the annotations you make, and the discussions you have are private unless you choose to share them. We do not sell your data. We do not show you advertising. We do not share your reading history with publishers, employers, or governments.
📖 Your reading history
What you read is stored securely and used only to improve your recommendations. It is never shared with third parties. You can delete your reading history at any time from your account settings.
🧠 Your soul profile
The results of your AI Reading Soul quiz are stored and used to power your book recommendations. They are never shared or sold. You can retake the quiz or delete your profile at any time.
💬 Your annotations and reviews
Private annotations are visible only to you. Public reviews are visible to other readers on the platform. You can change the visibility of any annotation or review, or delete it entirely.
💳 Payment information
Patron subscriptions are processed by Stripe. Global BookShelf never stores your full credit card number. Stripe handles all payment security and compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
🚫 No advertising, ever
Global BookShelf does not display advertising and does not allow advertisers to pay to have books promoted in your recommendations. Our revenue comes from the 5% platform fee on patron subscriptions — not from selling your attention.
📧 Email communications
We send you emails only when authors you follow publish new work, when your city launches on the platform, and for account-related notices. You can unsubscribe from any email at any time. We do not send promotional emails from third parties.
🗑️ Right to deletion
You can delete your Global BookShelf account at any time. Your reading history, soul profile, annotations and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Patron payments already processed cannot be reversed.
For questions about privacy, contact us at privacy@globalbookshelf.com. For data deletion requests, contact data@globalbookshelf.com.
