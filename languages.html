Things Fall Apart — Chapter 1 · Chinua Achebe (1958)
English — Original
Okonkwo was well known throughout the nine villages and even beyond. His fame rested on solid personal achievements. As a young man of eighteen he had brought honour to his village by throwing Amalinze the Cat. Amalinze was the great wrestler who for seven years was unbeaten, from Umuofia to Mbaino.
Swahili — Community translation
Okonkwo alikuwa maarufu katika vijiji vyote tisa na hata zaidi ya hapo. Umaarufu wake ulitegemea mafanikio yake binafsi. Akiwa kijana wa miaka kumi na nane, alileta heshima kwa kijiji chake kwa kumshinda Amalinze Paka. Amalinze alikuwa mshindani mkuu ambaye kwa miaka saba hakushindwa, kuanzia Umuofia hadi Mbaino.
